## Overview

This repository accompanies the paper:

> **A Parsimonious Three-Dimensional Representation of Customer Behavioral Variability**

The study proposes a parsimonious customer representation based on three complementary behavioral descriptors:

- Frequency
- Diversity
- Entropy

Using the Online Retail II dataset, the proposed representation is evaluated through:

- descriptive analysis
- correlation analysis
- K-means clustering
- Kruskal–Wallis tests
- Dunn's post-hoc tests
- managerial validation using observed customer revenue
- comparison with a conventional Frequency–Diversity representation

The results demonstrate that incorporating Entropy provides additional behavioral information beyond purchasing intensity and portfolio breadth, leading to stronger differentiation of customer revenue across behavioral segments.
